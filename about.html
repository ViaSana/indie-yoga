OB’s OG Hot Yoga Studio

Heat transforms — and in our hot yoga studio, it becomes an integral part of your journey. At Indie Yoga, you’re not stepping into a mere studio, but a home where every individual is cherished, every class is a collective journey, and every session brings you closer to your authentic self.

  • We Welcome All Levels: Our classes cater to both the novice and the experienced. Adjust your practice based on how you feel on any given day – challenge yourself or take it slow and steady.
  • All Our Classes are Heated: We heat our sessions with precision. While the intensity varies across classes, the warmth aids in muscle flexibility, enhances your practice, and ensures a revitalizing experience.
  • We Start and End Class on Time: Classes begin exactly as scheduled. We appreciate punctuality – ensure you're early to get the full Indie Yoga experience.
  • Our Studio Offers Mat Rentals and Shower Facilities: Need a mat? We’ve got you covered for a minimal fee. Plus, our shower facilities are available on a first-come basis.
